GENERAL INFORMATION ABOUT THIS MUSIC
---------------------------------------------------------------------------

"Operation Stealth - The Ballad of J. & J." (Duration/Playing Time: 01:35)
Copyright (C) 2006 Maxime Abbey
Version 1.0 (2006-07-01)

Here is my second release of year 2006, and, once again, a new tune from a
computer game, this game being "Operation Stealth", released as
"James Bond: The Stealth Affair" in the US, during the early 90s.
This music is the "underwater theme" of the game, and is mainly inspired by
the PC/MS-DOS/AdLib version of the original game soundtrack, although it
has also been inspired by the Amiga version.

The original soundtrack has been composed by Jean Baudlot. Many thanks
to him for this wonderful piece of music.

"Operation Stealth - The Ballad of J. & J."
